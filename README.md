## üî¨ Research Interests

My research group focuses on the intersection of control theory, machine learning, and diagnostics for complex systems. Key areas include:

* **Fault Detection, Diagnostics, and Prognostics (FDD&P):** Model-based and data-driven approaches for safety-critical systems.
* **Aerospace Systems:** Control and diagnostics for satellites, UAVs, and aircraft subsystems (e.g., reaction wheels, control moment gyros).
* **Intelligent Manufacturing & Industry 5.0:** Integrating AI and computer vision to improve manual labor efficiency and production monitoring.
* **Multi-Agent Systems:** Cooperative control and formation flying for autonomous vehicles (drones, satellites).
* **Control Theory:** Linear and nonlinear controller/observer design (Kalman filters, Reinforcement Learning).
